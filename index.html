Services

Mental check-up services are designed to assess and evaluate an individual's mental health and well-being. a series of assessments and interviews may be conducted to gather information about a person's thoughts, emotions, behaviors, and overall functioning.

Depression

Depression is a mental health condition characterized by persistent feelings of sadness, loss of interest or pleasure in activities, and a range of physical and emotional symptoms. It can affect a person's thoughts, behavior, and overall well-being. Depression can vary in severity, from mild to severe, and can have a significant impact on daily life and functioning. It is important to seek help and support from healthcare professionals if you or someone you know is experiencing symptoms of depression. Treatment options may include therapy, medication, lifestyle changes, and support from loved ones.

Anxiety

Anxiety is a natural response to stress or a perceived threat. It is a feeling of unease, worry, or fear that can vary in intensity from person to person. It often manifests as physical symptoms such as a rapid heart rate, sweating, trembling, or shortness of breath, along with psychological symptoms like racing thoughts, difficulty concentrating, and restlessness. It is important to note that while anxiety is a normal part of life, excessive or prolonged anxiety can be debilitating and may require professional help to manage.

Stress

Stress is a natural response to challenges or demands that we encounter in our lives. Caused by such as work pressure, relationship issues, or financial difficulties. It is important to identify the sources of stress and imple such as exercise, relaxation techniques, and time management, to effectively manage and reduce stress levels.

Eating disorder

An eating disorder is a mental health condition characterized by disturbances in eating habits and attitudes towards food, weight, and body image. It can involve behaviors such as restrictive eating, binge eating, or purging, and can have severe physical and psychological consequences.
